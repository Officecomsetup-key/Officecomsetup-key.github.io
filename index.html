Install Office Setup

  1. Sign in to Your Microsoft Account: Go to office/setup and sign in with your Microsoft account.
  2. Enter Your Product Key: Input the 25-digit alphanumeric Office setup product key.
  3. Select Country and Language: Choose your preferred country and language settings.
  4. Start Installation: Click on "Next" to begin the Office installation process.

Where to Find Your Microsoft Office Setup Product Key?: The Microsoft Office Setup Product Key is a combination of 25 alphanumeric characters. You can find it printed on the back side of your Microsoft Office Setup Card.

How to Proceed with Microsoft Office Setup Online

Here’s a step-by-step guide to setting up Microsoft Office using your 25-digit alphanumeric product key.

Example Product Key: ABD3M-GRT5B-45RFJ-W3R5Y-C5T9U

  1. Sign in to Your Office Account:
    • Go to office/setup
    • Sign in with your Microsoft account. If you don’t have an account, create a new one and use the same login credentials to sign into your My Office account.
  2. Redeem Your Product Key:
    • Enter your 25-digit product key (e.g., ABD3M-GRT5B-45RFJ-W3R5Y-C5T9U).
  3. Install Your Product:
    • After redeeming the product key, select "Install" for your product.
    • Click "Install" again. By default, this installs the 32-bit version of Office using the language selected when you redeemed the product. If you prefer the 64-bit version, change your language and version preferences before installing.
  4. Download the Installation File:
    • When you click "Install," a popup will appear.
    • In Internet Explorer, select “Run.”
    • In Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox, select “Save” to download the file. Once the download is complete, open the file manually to start the installation.
  5. Run the Installation:
    • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
    • You will receive a notification once the installation is complete, such as “You’re all set! Office is installed now.”

    An animated video will demonstrate how to locate Office applications on your computer. Follow the instructions provided, for example:

    • Click Start > All Apps to find your Office applications.
    • Select "Close" once you have followed the steps.
