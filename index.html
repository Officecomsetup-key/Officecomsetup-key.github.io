How do I set up Microsoft Office online?

  • First, sign in to your Office account at www.office.com/setup. Enter your product key to redeem it. If you don't have an account yet, create a new one and use the same Microsoft login credentials to sign in to your Office account.

  • Once you’ve completed this step, select "Install" for your product, and then select "Install" again. By default, this installs the 32-bit version of Office on your PC using the language you selected when you redeemed the product. If you prefer to install the 64-bit version or change the language, you can do so before proceeding.

  • After you click "Install," Internet Explorer will show you a popup with the option to “Run.” In Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox, you will get the option to save the file. Once the file is downloaded, open it manually and run the installation process.

  • You will be informed when the installation is complete with a message like “You’re all set! Office is installed now.” An animated video will then show you how to find Office applications on your computer. Follow the instructions in the window, for example, click Start > All Apps to see where your apps are, and then select Close. If you need more help, download the step-by-step Office Setup guide.
